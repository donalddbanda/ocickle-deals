How it works

Just 5 simple steps and your money is protected.

1. Create Trade

Login and click the create trade button. This will take you to a Create Trade Form

2. Fill the form

Fill in the required details such as seller information, add photos of cart items, total amount, and shipping details.

3. Share trade link

After creating the trade, share the generated trade link with the seller to proceed with the transaction.

4. Deposit funds

Deposit the total amount into the escrow account to secure the transaction.

5. Seller ships items

The seller will handover the item(s) to the courier you selected when entering trade details. Once the item(s) have been handedover to the courier, your funds will be released to the seller and the courier will handle the rest.
